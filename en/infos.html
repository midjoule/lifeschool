Inscription fees

Special Offer ! By pre-registering your child(ren) by March 31st 2023, you will benefit from an exceptional 15% discount on the school fees. This offer applies to newcomers only. It does not apply to children who have already gone through the registration process at our school.

We have chosen to create a financially accessible school so that all children, regardless of their parents' income, can benefit from the education and supportive environment that we offer.
The prices are calculated on the basis of a price list which takes into account several parameters of your tax return (e.g. net income, savings, number of children, etc.).
