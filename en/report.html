Activity report 2022-2023

A word from the director

This second year at Life School Riviera has enabled us to evolve and adapt, and to realize that the vision of our school is not necessarily understood by everyone, sometimes even by the families who have entrusted us with their children.

For this second year, we opted to open up the secondary section, as two of our pupils were transferring from 8H to 9H, and began the school year with 14 pupils divided between two classes.

Throughout the year, despite many challenges, we pursued our mission to accompany the children entrusted to us, preparing them to become fulfilled, responsible and respectful citizens.

I'll leave you to discover what our students and the whole school team have experienced during the 2022-2023 school year, in the form of commented illustrations.

Nathalie Rod Dumoulin, Founder of Life School Riviera
