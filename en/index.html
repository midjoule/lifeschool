Presentation

Life School Riviera is an innovative and progressive private school. It was born out of the desire to reveal each child's potential, talents and identity by offering quality teaching, tailor-made, adapted to the child's rhythm and learning profile. The heart of our school is to teach and transmit essential values, appreciation of others and sense of excellence. The school will open at the beginning of the school year 2021, in a green setting in Saint-Légier. Registration is open from 1H to 7H.

About us

We are parents, teachers and professionals who believe in the potential of every child.

The idea of ​​creating a different school on the Vaudoise Riviera seemed obvious to me as I watched the children around me. In every child there is a thirst for learning and the school should support parents in their role of encouraging their children to discover their interests, gifts and abilities.
We offer a place where children make new discoveries every day and grow at their own pace. We want them to operate in an environment that does not encourage competition but that encourages collaboration.

Nathalie Rod Dumoulin, Founder

Every child is unique
and deserves teaching that suits them

Vision

A well-taught child ensures his future and that of society.
We want a school where students are comfortable expressing themselves with who they are while feeling listened to and who can build their own identity and become responsible, respectful citizens as adults.
We also want a school where teachers are valued and rewarded for their investment.

Mission

Valuing each child by showing them that they are able to learn and are important to others.
Helping each child develop their potential.
Teaching children autonomy so that they can assume responsibilities and put into practice knowledge they have already acquired.
Encourage dialogue and positive contact between families and teachers.

Values

Listening, forgiveness, solidarity, self-esteem, respect, encouragement.
Our teachers must live these values ​​on a daily basis. We want to put into practice Christian values without any notion of religion or indoctrination.

Learning must have a meaning for the child.

Philosophy

We want to encourage each student and teacher to value the importance and reputation of the other and their family through respect and service.

We want to provide quality education that allows children to strive for excellence by giving their best without comparing themselves to others’ achievements. Excellence is part of our philosophy as we strive to get the best from our students at their own pace and ability.

We want a school anchored in a solid education where we give meaning to learning by allowing students to build their knowledge based on experience and then to use those skills and tools acquired in school in their everyday life.

We will follow the “Romand Study Plan” (RSP) and integrate Christian values ​​that are not religious. Our vision of the world is based on the Bible which reveals to us that human beings were created to have a relationship with God. However, this God who reaches out to us leaves us free to choose whether we want to believe in him or not. Christian values ​​allow each individual to make their own choices about their life and future.
